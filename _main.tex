\emph{Jan-March 2022}

This workbook contains details of the seven sessions given by \textbf{Chris Berry}:

\begin{itemize}
\item
  Week 26 (17/01/22). Simple regression.
\item
  Week 27 (24/01/22). Multiple regression: multiple continuous predictors
\item
  Week 28 (31/01/22). ANOVA: 2x2 Between-subjects
\item
  Week 29 (07/02/22). Multiple regression: one continuous, one categorical
\item
  Week 30 (14/02/22). Multiple regression: evaluating and comparing models
\item
  Week 31 - No session
\item
  Week 32 (28/02/22). ANOVA: Repeated measures
\item
  Week 33 (07/03/22). Pre-post data, effect sizes, clinically significant change
\end{itemize}
